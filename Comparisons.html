    Correlation

    This visualization shows a negative correlation between obesity and life expectancy. States with higher obesity rates tend to have lower average life expectancy, suggesting long-term health impacts at the population level. This scatter plot visualizes the strong inverse relationship between the two. We can conclude that obesity is a factor influencing life expectancy across states in the US.

    While this scatter plot suggests a negative correlation between air quality index (AQI) and life expectancy, the relationship between these two variable appears to be weaker. We are less conclusive about the relationship between AQI and Life Expectancy, but are still convinced that the two are inversely correlated. We also want to highlight that there is a smaller range of AQI in the United States while Obesity Rates vary more widely.
